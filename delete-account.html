Delete Your Account & Data

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This page explains how to delete your OrderMart account and the personal data associated with it. You can do this yourself in the app, or by contacting us.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

  1. Open the OrderMart app.
  2. Go to Account (the profile icon).
  3. Choose Delete Account and confirm.

Your account and personal profile are removed once you confirm.

Option 2 — Request by email

Send an email from your registered email address to Ayadmaulod22@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my OrderMart account". We will verify and process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile information — name, email, phone number, and delivery address (community, floor, house).
  • Your cart and saved preferences.

What we may keep

We may retain limited order and transaction records for a short period where required for accounting, legal, or fraud-prevention purposes. These records are deleted or anonymized once no longer needed.
