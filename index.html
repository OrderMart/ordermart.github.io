Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know about ordering with OrderMart.

What is OrderMart?

OrderMart is a mobile app that lets you order from your local market and pharmacy and have it delivered to your door in 20 to 30 minutes.

How do I order groceries online with OrderMart?

Download the OrderMart app, register your community, browse your local market, add items to your cart, and place your order for fast delivery.

How fast is OrderMart delivery?

Most orders are delivered within 20 to 30 minutes of placing them.

Can I order medicine and pharmacy products?

Yes. You can order pharmacy and everyday health products from your local pharmacy alongside your groceries, all in one app.

How do I pay for my order?

OrderMart uses Cash on Delivery, so you pay when your order arrives. No card is required.

Which areas does OrderMart deliver to?

OrderMart serves residential communities and is expanding across Iraq. Contact us to check whether your community is covered.

Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery is fast and affordable. The exact fee for your community is shown at checkout before you confirm your order.

How do I create an OrderMart account?

Sign up in the OrderMart app with your phone number and choose your community to start ordering.

How can I bring OrderMart to my community?

If you own a market or pharmacy, see our membership plans or contact us to get set up.
